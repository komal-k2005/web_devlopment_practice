Services

Application Development and Maintenance: Hexaware helps businesses develop, enhance, and maintain custom software applications to meet specific business requirements. They provide services for various technologies and platforms. Enterprise Solutions: Hexaware offers enterprise resource planning (ERP) solutions, customer relationship management (CRM) systems, and other enterprise-level software solutions to streamline business operations and improve efficiency. Digital Transformation: Hexaware assists businesses in their digital transformation journey by leveraging emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence (AI), machine learning (ML), robotic process automation (RPA), and cloud computing. Quality Assurance and Testing: Hexaware provides comprehensive testing services to ensure the quality, reliability, and security of software applications. This includes functional testing, performance testing, security testing, and test automation. Infrastructure Management Services: Hexaware offers infrastructure management services to help businesses optimize their IT infrastructure, including server management, network management, data center services, and cloud infrastructure management. Business Process Services: Hexaware provides business process outsourcing (BPO) services to handle various back-office operations, including finance and accounting, human resources, procurement, and supply chain management.
